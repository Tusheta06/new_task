About Us

Think global, Act local.

NayePankh Foundation is one of the biggest student-led non-governmental organisations in India. Formed with a strong desire to serve society and make the world a better place, we strive to make our vision successful through direct grassroot actions.

Service to mankind is service to God. We believe that if we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve. Join us to revolutionise society together!
